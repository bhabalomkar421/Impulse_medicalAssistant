Common Medical Emergencies

1. Bleeding
    - unable to control the bleeding after first aid
    - Deep wound
    - Object in wound
    - Bone or tissue is visible

2. Breathing Difficulties
    - Pain when taking deep breaths
    - Noisy breathing or wheezing
    - Chest is tight

3. Fainting
    - lost consiousness

4. Fits/Seizures

5. Severe Pain

6. Heart Attack
    - Chest Pain
    - Dizziness
    - Sweating

7. Stroke
    - Changes in face (eyes, mouth)
    - problem while lifting arms or numbness
    - Changed Speech
    - Understand and repond
    
8. Burns
    - Acid or scalds

9. Electrocution
    - Severe or deep burns at high voltages
